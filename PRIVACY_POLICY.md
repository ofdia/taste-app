# Privacy Policy for Ramenlog

Effective date: 2026-03-15

Ramenlog respects your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

Ramenlog stores the information you choose to enter into the app, including:

- shop names
- menu names
- ratings
- review text
- visit dates
- ramen preference options
- photos you choose to attach

## 2. How Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide the core features of the app, such as:

- saving ramen records
- showing favorites and revisit lists
- displaying personal statistics
- exporting and restoring backups

## 3. Data Storage

Ramenlog stores your data locally on your device.

At this time, Ramenlog does not require account creation and does not upload your personal ramen logs to a remote server as part of the core app experience shown in this project.

## 4. Photos and Permissions

If you choose to attach photos to a ramen entry, the app will request permission to access your photo library. This permission is used only so you can select images to add to your own records.

## 5. Data Sharing

Ramenlog does not sell your personal data.

Ramenlog does not share your personal ramen log data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Ramenlog may display ads served by Google AdMob. Ad providers may process advertising identifiers, device
information, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads.

## 6. Backups

If you choose to export a backup, the exported backup content is created from your local app data and shared only through actions you explicitly initiate.

## 7. Children

Ramenlog is not directed to children under 13.
