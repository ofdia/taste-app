# Ramenlog Play Store Listing

## App Name

Ramenlog

## Short Description

Record ramen visits, photos, ratings, and favorite shops in one clean log.

## Full Description

Ramenlog is a personal ramen journal for tracking every bowl you try.

Save each visit with the shop name, menu, rating, visit date, photo, and review. You can also keep track of ramen preferences like broth strength, noodle firmness, and oil level.

Ramenlog helps you:

- Log ramen visits quickly
- Save photos and notes for each bowl
- Track ratings from 1 to 5
- Mark favorite shops for future visits
- Browse records by list or photo view
- Review your ramen history in one place
- Export and restore your data with backup support

Whether you are exploring new ramen shops or keeping a personal list of repeat favorites, Ramenlog gives you a simple way to remember what you ate and where you want to go again.
